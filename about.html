With a history rooted in the Chumash people and later Spanish and Mexican influences, the city has grown into a diverse and thriving community. Santa Maria is celebrated as a top producer of strawberries and wine grapes, contributing to its reputation as a culinary and wine destination.

Visitors and residents alike enjoy its scenic landscapes, mild Mediterranean climate, and attractions like the Pacific Conservatory Theatre (PCPA) and nearby beaches. Whether exploring its historical roots or savoring its famous tri-tip barbecue, Santa Maria offers a unique blend of tradition and modern charm.
